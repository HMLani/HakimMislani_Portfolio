'Back to Present' VR Escape Room

Unity VR Development C# Game Design

Puzzle Design

Each room features distinct mechanics and progressive difficulty, ensuring an engaging and varied gameplay experience that challenges players' problem-solving skills.

Augmented Reality Business Card

Unity Vuforia AR Development Adobe Illustrator

An innovative business card that comes to life through augmented reality. Users can scan the physical card with their phone to view interactive 3D content and additional information.

Technical Implementation

  • Design Optimization: Multiple iterations to achieve 5-star Vuforia rating
  • AR Integration: Unity + Vuforia for seamless tracking and rendering
  • Mobile Deployment: Android APK for real-device testing
  • Image Recognition: Advanced natural feature detection

The project required careful consideration of contrast, feature distribution, and visual elements to ensure optimal AR tracking performance across different lighting conditions.
